13.11.2006

Glitnir Bank issues USD 500 million of Floating Rate Notes

Glitnir Bank issued $500 million of Floating Rate Notes on 10 November. The notes, due in January 2011, were issued in response to notable demand from institutional investors in the US. The deal pays a margin of 44 basis points over 3 month US$ LIBOR with an issue price of par. The offering was the first to be made off Glitnir's recently-signed US$ 5 billion US Medium Term Note Programme, which was arranged by JP Morgan Securities.
